KOHINOOR INTERNATIONAL ACADEMY was established in 2008. In village Tatiana, Cheeka-Patiala Road, (Haryana) its campus spread on a vast area of 41/2 Acres, the school fulfils all the requirements of the modern education system. Besides having fully furnished 56 spacious class rooms, it has well equipped composite lab, S.st. Lab, Maths Lab, Audio-Visual Lab, computer Lab, Chemistry Lab, Physics Lab, Bio Lab, & Special rooms such as Music Room, Sports Room, Medical Room & divinity room, a library with number of books & a multipurpose hall with a capacity of 2000 students &the administrative block to smooth run the administrative works. Keeping in view the importance of outdoor games, the school maintains two vast playground with all facilities. The school is situated in pollution free & noise free environment. The atmosphere of school is serene & tranquil. The school building is lushed with green lawns which blooms with different colourful flowers seasonally. The students enjoy their study in the lap of nature.
